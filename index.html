Let's Play YouTube Channel

One of my hobbies is maintaining a Let's Play Youtube channel, in which I only have one goal: post at least one video everyday, or at least seven videos a week. I've enjoyed teaching myself how to use recording and editing software such as Open Broadcaster Software and Adobe Premiere Pro.
Side benefits of this project include being a better impromptu speaker and learning about fair use, copyright law and the psychology of content producers and consumers.
